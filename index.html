Troubleshooting

If you have a problem try to remember how it happened so we can see if you can do it your self or need to bring it in the shop! If you need to bring it in the shop it is also good to know when, how and what happened so its easier for our experts to fix your item.

Common Issues

When there is issues with electronics sometimes its from the users side. From reading the instructions, to how to use it. Here is a list of common issues with solutions:

  • Issue: Not turning on? Solution: make sure all the cables are plugged in if it has any, is it charged?
  • Issue: Not sure hoe to use it? Solution: sometimes you will need to look at the boring "user guides"
  • Issue: Not connecting to wifi? Solution try to reset your router by unplugging it for ~30 seconds then plug it in and try again.
